Research

I envision having AI imitate the human cognition process across modalities. This is, I work with models with different types of inputs to reduce the gap between human and machine cognition. Currently, I am focusing on projects in Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision. Specifically, I focus on Language Disambiguation and Visual Cognition/Understanding.
