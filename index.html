Research

I focus on having AI imitate the human brain. This is, I work with generative models with different types of inputs to reduce the gap between human and machine cognition. Currently, I am working on multiple projects in Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Robotics. Specifically, I focus on AI alignment, diffusion models, and robot dexterity.
